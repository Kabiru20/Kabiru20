## Approach & Philosophy

I approach data problems by first understanding the data-generating
process and research question, then designing databases, pipelines,
and models that are appropriate in terms of assumptions, performance,
and maintainability.

I value:
- Sound statistical reasoning over unnecessary complexity  
- Clear schemas and queries over ad-hoc data access  
- Diagnostics and validation over blind optimization  
- Reproducibility and clarity over one-off analysis  

Models, databases, and pipelines are tools; insight, reliability,
and rigor are the objective.
